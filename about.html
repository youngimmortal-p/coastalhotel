Our Story

Coastal Stay was founded on the dream of bringing serenity and sophistication together by the sea. Every corner of our hotel is crafted to reflect elegance, comfort, and natural beauty. Whether you’re here for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a peaceful retreat, we promise an unforgettable stay surrounded by ocean views and timeless luxury.

Mission

To offer unforgettable experiences through personalized hospitality and genuine care.

Vision

To be Africa’s most loved and trusted luxury coastal retreat.

Values

Sustainability, Excellence, Integrity, and Comfort are at the heart of everything we do.
